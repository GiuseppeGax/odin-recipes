Odins Famouse Cranberry and Almond Banana Breada

10 Ingredients / Baking Time 1hr/ Prep Time 10min / 4.7/5 Rating

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanila extract
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Almonds
  • Cranberry
    •

    Baking

    1. In a stand mixer mix, butter, vanilla extract, sugar and eggs until fluffy
    2. In a bowl mix flour, cinnamon, nutmeg
    3. Squash a banana inside a bowl
    4. Turn oven on bake to 350 Farenheit
    5. Slowly add the flour mix, almonds, squashed banana, and cranberries into the standmixer
    6. Spread into mold (for an extra touch cut a banana in half and lay on top)
    7. Put inside oven and bake for 1 hour or until cooked too the center
    8. Let rest for 15 minutes and enjoy!
