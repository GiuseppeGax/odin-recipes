Odins Spicy Devil Eggsa

6 Ingredients / Baking Time 12in/ Prep Time 10min / 5/5 Rating

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking

  1. Add salt and boil 4 cups of water inside a pan
  2. Add eggs into boilign water and let boil for 10-12 minutes
  3. Make an Icebath while eggs boil
  4. Remove eggs and place inside ice bath for 5 minutes
  5. Remove the shell and cut in half
  6. Inside a bag add mayonnaise, salt, pepper, and the boiled egg yolk mix and then pipe into eggs
  7. Serve! and enjoy
