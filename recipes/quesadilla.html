Odins Cheesy & Beefy Quessadilla

4 Ingredients / Baking Time 10in/ Prep Time 5min / 4.8/5 Rating

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Ground beef
  • American Cheese
  • Montreal seasoning

Cooking

  1. Cook Ground beef till crispy
  2. Heat up tortilla and add ground beef and american cheese
  3. Place weight ontop of quesadilla and cook both ides for 3 minutes or until cheese melted and torilla crispy
  4. Serve and enjoy
